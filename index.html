Alien Sweeper

The aliens have invaded! We need you to hunt them down before they destroy us!

Aliens are hiding on the board. Click on a square to see if an alien is hiding there. If you click on a square containing an alien, you lose. If you click all the squares (without clicking on any aliens) you win!

Clicking a square which doesn't have an alien reveals the number of neighbouring squares containing bombs.

To open a square, point at the square and click on it. To mark a square you think contains an alien, point and Shift+Click.
